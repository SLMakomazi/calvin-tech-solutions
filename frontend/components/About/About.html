About Calvin Tech Solutions

100% Black-Owned | B-BBEE Level 1 | 100% Young-Owned

Calvin Tech Solutions is a 100% Black and Young-owned, B-BBEE Level 1 technology company based in Stellenbosch, South Africa. Founded by a passionate young entrepreneur from Kayamandi, we are on a mission to bridge the digital divide and empower businesses through practical, scalable, and affordable technology solutions.

We specialize in helping small and medium-sized businesses transition into the digital age by providing modern systems, automation tools, and business-driven technology strategies that enhance efficiency, visibility, and long-term growth.
